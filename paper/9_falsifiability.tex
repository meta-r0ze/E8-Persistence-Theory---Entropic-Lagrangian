\section{Falsifiability: Structural Kill-Switches}
\label{sec:falsifiability}

Unlike Effective Field Theories, which can accommodate new particles by adding Lagrangian terms, the Entropic Lagrangian is structurally closed. The integers $\mathbb{S} = \{\Delta, \nu, \sigma, \chi\}$ completely saturate the degrees of freedom. Therefore, the theory presents specific ``Hard Constraints'' that serve as immediate falsification criteria:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{The Spin-2 Limit (No Supergravity):} The lattice projection capacity ($D=4$) strictly prohibits fundamental fields with spin $>2$. The discovery of a spin-3/2 gravitino or higher-spin gauge field would refute the manifold projection constraint.
    
    \item \textbf{The Perturbative Gravity Prohibition:} We derive General Relativity as an emergent hydrodynamic limit (System VI). This requires that gravity be asymptotically safe or non-perturbative in the UV. The discovery that gravity follows standard perturbative quantization (like QED) at the Planck scale would falsify the bulk regulator derivation.
    
    \item \textbf{The Fourth Generation Prohibition:} The chiral channel capacity is saturated at $\nu=16$ (3 generations + neutrinos). The discovery of a 4th generation of fermions would imply $\nu > 16$, falsifying the $E_8 \to D_4 \oplus D_4$ substrate hypothesis.
    
    \item \textbf{Lorentz Violation at Low Energy:} The derivation of the Metric Signature relies on the Bandwidth Limit ($c_{eff}=1$). Any experimental confirmation of Lorentz violation (e.g., energy-dependent speed of light) at energies $E \ll M_P$ would invalidate the channel capacity argument.

    \textbf{Dynamical Dark Energy ($\Lambda$ Evolution):} The Margin pillar strictly defines the Cosmological Constant as a static, non-derivative geometric baseline cost required to resolve the spatial manifold. Any confirmed detection of a time-varying equation of state ($w \neq -1$) or dynamically evolving dark energy would falsify the identification of $\Lambda$ as the static Margin cost.
    
    \item \textbf{The Lattice Stiffness Check ($\kappa \neq 1$):} The derivation of Gravity as a Goldstone mode closes the Einstein-Hilbert limit algebraically, but requires non-perturbative numerical confirmation. Constructing the Transfer Matrix $\hat{T}(\beta)$ of the $E_8$ substrate and computing the traceless-transverse correlator $C_{TT}(k)$ via Monte Carlo simulation must yield a dimensionless stiffness ratio converging to exactly unity:
\begin{equation*}
\kappa \equiv \frac{C_{TT}(k_{ref})}{(M_P^2/32\pi) k_{ref}^2} \to 1 \pm \mathcal{O}(1/\beta)
\end{equation*}
    A deviation $\kappa \neq 1$ (outside finite-size scaling errors) would imply that lattice artifacts disrupt the Goldstone mode, explicitly falsifying the emergence of General Relativity from this specific discrete substrate.

    \textbf{The Prohibition of Other Spins:} The substrate geometry strictly prohibits fundamental particles with spin $> 2$, providing a structural mechanism for the Weinberg-Witten theorem:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{No Spin-3/2 (Gravitinos):} A spin-3/2 field requires a vector-spinor coupling. As established in the substrate projection, this cannot be constructed from the orthogonal $D_4 \oplus D_4$ decomposition without introducing geometrically suppressed mirror fermions. This structurally falsifies Supersymmetry as a fundamental property of the vacuum.
    \item \textbf{No Spin $\ge$ 3:} A fundamental spin-3 field in 4D spacetime requires a totally symmetric rank-3 tensor, which possesses 20 independent components. Because the fundamental chiral bit-depth of the lattice node is strictly limited to $\nu=16$ \cite{meyer_informational_2026}, the substrate literally lacks the hardware channel capacity to encode or propagate a fundamental spin-3 state. Higher-spin extensions are therefore physically forbidden by Shannon capacity limits.
\end{itemize}

\end{enumerate}
